Nepal Adarsha Awasiya Vidalaya, an abode of academic excellence, is an English medium Private Boarding School that lies on the outskirts of mesmerizing Pokhara Valley, nearly 11 km. southeast of main downtown and Pokhara airport. Since its establishment in the year 2052 B.S. (1996 A.D.), it has been inculcating quality education to all knowledge aspiring students and those parents whose main impulse is to explore the underlying potential talent in their children.

The school's beyond comparison in terms of its extension. It occupies an area of 7.36 (57 Ropanis) Acres of land and is situated away from a daily commotion and pollution free environment.
